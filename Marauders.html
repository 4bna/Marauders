About Marauders

The Marauders are an elite Third-Person Shooter (TPS) clan, owned by KingNinjah. Assisting him are the Prince - gunfein, civaltoviann, and Xeqtricity. The Royalty includes deadharry, T_nacity, icey4a, D_wrldz, Thalonis, and UnstableExistence.

The Marauders are known for their exceptional skills and teamwork, with the clan being carried by the GOAT, endcharr.

Current Divisions

The Marauders are divided into three elite divisions:

  • Scorch Guard: The main scrim players, consisting of highly skilled individuals.
  • Reaper Guard: Elite players who assist Scorch Guard members in scrims. They are the best of the best and consist of only a few top-tier players.
  • Palace Guard: The backbone of the clan, providing support and ensuring smooth operations.
