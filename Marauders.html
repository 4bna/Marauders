About Marauders

The Marauders are an elite Third-Person Shooter (TPS) clan, owned by KingNinjah. Assisting him are the Prince - gunfein, civaltoviann, and Xeqtricity. The Royalty includes deadharry, T_nacity, icey4a, D_wrldz, Thalonis, and UnstableExistence.

The Marauders are known for their exceptional skills and teamwork, with the clan being carried by the GOAT, endcharr.
